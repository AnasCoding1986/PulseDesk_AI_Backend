# Screenshot & Thumbnail Guide 📸

To make PulseDesk AI stand out in your portfolio (Upwork/Behance), follow this capture strategy.

## 1. The Hero Shot (Thumbnail)
**Page**: `Landing Page`
**View**: Hero section with the "Dashboard Preview" partially visible.
**Goal**: Capture the gradient text and the floating stats. Use a browser-frame mockup if possible.

## 2. The Operational Command (Primary)
**Page**: `Dashboard Overview`
**View**: Full screen showing the metrics bar, revenue chart, and AI insights feed.
**Goal**: Show the "density" of valuable data.

## 3. The Project Workflow
**Page**: `Projects -> Kanban Board`
**View**: Capture while dragging a card (mid-air) to show the drag-overlay effect.
**Goal**: Demonstrate high-quality interaction design.

## 4. The Client Experience
**Page**: `Client Portal`
**View**: The "NovaTech Platform Redesign" timeline.
**Goal**: Highlight the "External-facing" value of the software.

## 5. The AI Intelligence
**Page**: `Reports`
**View**: The "Report Preview" after generation.
**Goal**: Show the AI summary cards (Wins, Risks) and the typewriter effect.

---

## 💡 Capture Tips:
- **Resolution**: Use 1440p if possible for crisp text.
- **Data**: Ensure the seeded data is visible (avoid empty states for portfolio shots).
- **Dark Mode**: PulseDesk is optimized for dark mode; ensure no blue-light filters are on during capture.
- **Mockups**: Use tools like `Screenity` or `Shots.so` to wrap screenshots in high-quality device frames.
